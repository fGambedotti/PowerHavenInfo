Vision

Datacentres should not be forced to buy most energy at the most expensive hours.

Power Haven helps operators reshape demand, use on-site batteries intelligently, and run more resiliently under UK grid constraints.

Product

What VoltPilot does on site.

Measure flexibility

Create an auditable baseline across UPS, cooling and storage so operators can see usable flexibility without guesswork.

Optimise demand decisions

Coordinate dispatch-ready behaviour against market and system signals while maintaining datacentre performance standards.

Support compliant participation

Produce structured operational evidence that supports engagement with energy and infrastructure requirements.
